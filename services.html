Recommended 3D Printing Services

I don’t offer prints myself, but these services will help you bring my designs to life with top-notch quality and fast turnaround.

JLCPCB (PLC3DP)

I've ordered PCBs from JLCPCB and they've been absolutely top-tier every time—fast, reliable, and consistent. Their 3D printing division (PLC3DP) offers a wide range of materials, finishes, and incredible precision for serious projects.

Visit JLCPCB

Shapeways

While I haven’t used them personally (yet), Shapeways is highly reviewed for metal 3D printing and chrome-like finishes. They offer metal deposition, polishing, and plating services that are ideal for getting that OEM-quality emblem look.

If my experience changes, I’ll update this recommendation accordingly.

Visit Shapeways
